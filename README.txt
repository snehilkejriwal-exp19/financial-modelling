# Financial Modelling — Public Companies

## Objective
To build Excel-based 3-statement financial models for public companies,
analysing Income Statements, Balance Sheets, and Cash Flow Statements
to compute key financial ratios and identify business health trends.

## What This Project Covers
- 3-statement model construction with fully linked cross-sheet formula references
- Income Statement analysis — Gross Margin, EBIT Margin, Net Margin
- Balance Sheet analysis — Shareholders Equity, Debt/Equity Ratio
- Cash Flow analysis — FCF, FCF Margin, FCF Conversion Rate
- Executive Summary dashboard with trend analysis across 3 years

## Companies Modelled
| Company | Period | Key Metrics |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| PayPal Holdings (PYPL) | 2023–2025 | FCF +31.85%, EBIT Margin expanding to 19.28% |

## Key Findings — PayPal Holdings
- Revenue grew 11.4% from $29.7B to $33.2B — steady, consistent growth
- All margins expanding across 3 years — company becoming more operationally efficient
- FCF grew 31.85%, outpacing revenue growth — strong cash generation
- Gross Margin stable at ~46% across all 3 years — pricing power intact
- Debt/Equity ratio rising slightly (45.96% → 49.30%) — minor concern to monitor
